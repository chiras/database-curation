NCBI ITS2 vascular plant curation

Here is a list of the result of the database curation for ITS2 nucleotide sequences for vascular plants. This serves as an overview of which sequences passed our curation filters, and made it into the final databases.
The code repository is available at https://github.com/chiras/database-curation. The corresponding article is available as preview while being under review: https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2023.06.12.544582v1 Final curated databases are available at:
  • (i) global database: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7968519;
  • (ii) crop database: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7969940, and
  • (iii) country-level databases for the 27 EU countries: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7970046.
Filtering options allow to search for specific taxa, per default “Malus ” is set. You can change this to look up species or accessions of your interest.
